# Product Vision & Positioning

The DES Model Registry is a free, open, community-curated catalog of discrete event simulation models — a "Netflix for DES" where practitioners from any domain can discover, learn from, and contribute simulation model blueprints.

## Core Value Proposition

DES modeling is part art, part science. The registry captures the collective wisdom of the simulation community in a vendor-neutral format. AI helps scale content creation, but human practitioners vet and refine entries — ensuring the "art" is preserved alongside the science.

## Positioning

- **Standalone community resource first, Quodsi brand asset second.** The registry exists to democratize DES knowledge, not to sell software.
- **Vendor-neutral.** Model entries link out to implementations in any DES software (AnyLogic, Simio, Arena, Quodsi, etc.) as equals.
- **Open access.** All content is public. No paywall, no gated content.
- **Wikipedia-inspired editorial model.** Low barrier to start an entry, community enriches over time.

## Relationship to Quodsi

The registry is a separate website from the Quodsi diagramming plugin product. Quodsi appears as one of potentially many linked implementations for a given model. The registry builds Quodsi's brand credibility and community goodwill without being a sales funnel.
